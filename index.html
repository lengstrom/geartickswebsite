FTC 6055 Gearticks: Home

Our club participates in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), an intense robotics competition that requires great commitment from team members. The ultimate goal of FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) and by extension of this club is to educate the leaders of tomorrow in the engineering process and to teach students to innovate to solve both small and global problems. Last year we made it to the state finals as a rookie team, winning multiple awards on the way.

Please explore our site to learn more about us.

We are learning many new skills in FTC. The first, and one of the most important is how to use tools safely. There are fourteen people on our team this year and the meetings tend to get very hectic, so we are learning to stay on task.
